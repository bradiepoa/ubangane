The history of Ubangane ventures limited in the world

Ubungane Ventures Ltd is one of Uganda's leading exporters of natural vanilla and cocoa beans. The company offers a variety of high-quality aromatic natural vanilla products from different districts of Uganda like Kasese, Mukono, Buikwe, Bundibugyo, Fortportal, Rubirizi, and Masaka. The vanilla and cocoa products include vanilla beans, powder, extract, paste, vanilla cuts, vanilla caviar, and cocoa beans, cocoa powder, cocoa butter, respectively.

bungane's product catalog is a clasic addition to the client's product range because of the guaranteed price variations over a set period of time, experience within the vanilla industry characterised by the technical knowledge in the food and flavor industry.
